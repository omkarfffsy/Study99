Student Code of Conduct

Commit to your success today.

Welcome to Study Hub. To get the maximum benefit from these resources, please agree to the following guidelines:

  1. Daily Consistency: I commit to attending my scheduled lectures and sticking to my study timetable.
  2. Active Note-Taking: I will maintain dedicated notebooks and actively take notes.
  3. Practice & Revision: I will solve the provided DPPs and review formulas regularly.
  4. Zero Distractions: I will turn off notifications and avoid switching to social media.

Success is the sum of small efforts, repeated day in and day out.
